Our Mission

Our mission is to inform people of South Korea and showcase the amazing things to do. There is amazing food, marketplaces, tourist attractions, and more. We are trying to bring more awareness to what South Korea can offer for people visiting for the first time.

We also want to spread the many wonderful things about Korean Culture. Over the past few years, K-pop has grown massively world-wide and through that, Korean Culture has also been widespread. We want to expand on that and give more information to people who are genuinely interested in our culture and traditions.
